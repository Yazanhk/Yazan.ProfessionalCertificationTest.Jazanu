Question 1: Which TWO of the following are characteristics of a hot site disaster recovery strategy? (Select 2)
A) Lowest cost recovery option
B) Fully operational with real-time data mirroring
C) Takes weeks to become operational
D) Near-instantaneous failover capability
E) Requires hardware installation after disaster
F) Data synchronized periodically, not real-time
Correct Answers: B and D
Explanation: A hot site is a fully operational backup facility with identical hardware/software and real-time data synchronization. It provides near-instantaneous failover with minimal data loss. Cold sites (A, C, E) are basic facilities requiring hardware installation and taking days/weeks to become operational. Warm sites (F) have periodic synchronization and take hours to days.

Detective (A) identifies attacks during occurrence, Corrective (C) operates after an attack, compensating (E) substitutes for principal controls, and Directive (F) enforces rules of behavior.
